Our Mission

To deliver precise player development to all students of the game, and provide the opportunity for young, dedicated athletes, to learn to play the game of Basketball at a highly competitive level. We are committed to each athlete, no matter what their experience; assisting in the growth and development of athletic, educational, and social skills. We firmly promote sportsmanship, ethics, and overall positive lessons both on and off the court.

PLAYER DEVELOPMENT

90 minutes of intense, richly educational basketball from an expert instructor. Coach Ward is known for his Honesty, Player Evaluations, and Real Time Feedback. All communication is personal and verbal in person or over the phone. Proper skills, offensive/defensive techniques, proper offensive/defensive footwork. Everything is taught the way today’s game is being played. European open floor influence - Continuity Ball-Screen Offense and how to guard it defensively.

AAU

The CWB AAU program is made up of our own Player Development / Camp athletes. WE DO NOT RECRUIT! We teach our student athletes how to play the game, allowing for a smooth transition into our highly competitive AAU Program. Several College Coaches at all three levels DI, DII, DIII have stated that CWB is the ONLY PROGRAM on the East Coast that plays at this AAU Level without recruitment or open tryout. Overall winning percentage in the program is 65% at a Regional/National Level. Virtually ALL CWB Tournaments are Collegiate Exposure Tournaments. Our Staff is second to none, with the players best interest and development in the heart of every practice/game. We play in the most competitive live events available, allowing our players vast exposure to College Coaches. CWB takes its athletes extremely seriously and always has the needed time to discuss progress with parents. Reaching your goal is our number one priority.

ABOUT

Coach Ward created CWB as a platform to highlight the importance of fundamental skills in the "end game" of a highly competitive sport. Coach Ward's honest approach to the overall basketball experience, both on and off the floor, has made him one of a kind as a Player Development Specialist. He has worked with hundreds of players in the area, some of whom travel over 2 hours to work with him. He has placed over 75 of his students into collegiate basketball programs and continues to build players at any level. Coaching for over 32 years, ranging from HS to College DII assistant, his knowledge and passion for the game is second to none. He is well known for establishing & rebuilding programs throughout New York, New Jersey, and South Carolina.
